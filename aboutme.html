My Bio

I am a UX/UI designer who has a keen sense of aesthetic intuitive design. I have a BA in Graphic Design, 3.83 GPA from Western Connecticut State University in Danbury, CT. I started in graphic design and found that I had a huge passion for designing interfaces to create important experiences that solve problems for users. I am a first-generation Italian-American designer from Westchester, New York.

I have a background in traditional graphic design but transitioned to User Experience because of my driven ability to solve both user and business problems.
